VT Miltope Data Sheets

With the releasing of new rugged storage and network peripheral models for the aviation industry, this B2B company decided their existing product data sheets were due for a update and a refresh.

Design

The design of these data sheets was inspired by an existing print advertisement already printed in aviation industry targeted magazines. Working close with a local photographer, professional product photography was added to complement the strategically styled information design. Resulting in eye pleasing, easy reference data sheets for products and their technical specifications.
