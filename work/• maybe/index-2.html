SVOD Theme Development

With the shift in focus from transactional sales to a subscription based sales model, a big change was set in place for the themes on the Denver based VOD platform.

My Role

In 2016 I was asked to move from my marketing focused position and take on the sole responsibility of the platform's themes, as the new Creative Developer.

In this role I am responsible for the design, maintenance and the development of themes. I also lead the UI/UX efforts to continually improve the customer's experience and I work close with stakeholders to identify user needs.

The Challenge

Though the initial assignment was simple — add more attractive themes on the platform — it quickly became clear that currently the themes were too expensive, both in development time and in maintenance time, with very little commonality between them.
