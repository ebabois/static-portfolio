Home Work

SVOD Theme Development

With the shift in focus from transactional sales to a subscription based sales model, a big change was set in place for the themes on the Denver based VOD platform.

Introduction

My Role

In 2016 I was asked to move from my marketing focused position and take on the sole responsibility of the platform's themes, as the new Creative Developer.

In this role I am responsible for the design, maintenance and the development of themes. I also lead the UI/UX efforts to continually improve the customer's experience and I work close with stakeholders to identify user needs.

The Challenge

...ready, ...set, ...HOLD ON?!

Though the initial assignment was simple — add more attractive themes on the platform — it quickly became clear that currently the themes were too expensive, both in development time and in maintenance time, with very little commonality between them.

"themes were too expensive, both in development time and in maintenance time, with very little commonality between them"

The Approach

First things, first.

During competitor research we looked at some of the big players in the website and e-commerce theme space, to get a feel for the industry standard for theme structure and development. By normalizing the structure of the themes and their assets into a master theme structure (similar to the parent/child theme concept in the website theme space), both the development and the maintenance of these new themes would be reduced dramatically.

But how do we get there?

  • Stabilize: Find and fix crucial bugs in legacy themes.
  • Research: Establish master theme and design
  • Development: Develop new master themes with model variations
  • Retire: Move existing clients off legacy themes.

Predictable and Familiar

By normalizing the pages that are involved with finding, purchasing, consuming content and account management the Master User Flow is established. This user flow aims to honor the principles of Nielsen’s Heuristics to ensure a pleasant, predictable experience for the user.

"This user flow aims to honor the principles of Nielsen’s Heuristics to ensure a pleasant, predictable experience for the user"

Flexible

This Master User Flow also needs to be able to respond to the different variations possible depending on pricing type, product type and delivery type. These variations have been documented in the Master User Flow research process and the expected theme behavior based on these preferences specified by the client.

Design & Testing

Testing Performance

Conversions

While some themes are converting much higher than the 2.3% benchmark, we are putting an effort in place to improve these conversion rates.

So far we have found that these numbers are highly influenced by the following factors:

  • What theme is it?
  • What client is it?
  • How good is their (external) marketing effort?
  • How good is their price point?

This insight does not only allow us to finetune the themes for better conversion numbers, it also allows us to set our clients up for success by sharing this data with them.

Prototype

The Powerful Intelivideo Master Structure

Attractive themes that are flexible enough to be a delivery website and powerful enough to be the client’s primary website, strategically built to create a deep sense of loyalty with their subscribers. Clients will love how easy it is to build successful businesses on the platform, their customers will love using their websites allowing it to play an important part of their daily life.

With the stable master theme in place, maintenance is much easier, asset requirements between themes are consistent and functionaility is easily added, even retro-actively. Development of new themes is down by as much as 75% in development time.

"Development of new themes is down by up to 75% in development time."

Designed to Convert

Focused on conversions and retention by creating customer loyalty, by adhering to the industry’s best pratices, predictable user experience and transparency.

Actively Audited and Maintained

Striving to always improve the user's experience we perform User Testing, monitor support tickets and are actively working on putting A/B testing in place.

Responsive

With a Lean UX approach we spend less time on producing prototypes and more time on developing new themes and releasing usability improvements faster.
