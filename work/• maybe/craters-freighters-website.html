Craters & Freighters Website Redesign

This company has been in business for more than 20 years, and offers everything a shipment would require, yet their leads were not quite as they would like them to be.

Design

Even though the items shipped will vary greatly in size and value our target audience has the same goal, having their item shipped and handled with care. Craters & Freighters have been in business for over 20 years and were in need of a website that was easy to use and has a comprehensive infrastructure.

As Brand Standards were already in place for this crating & shipping company, I did not only design a usable site, but also one that compliments and extends the identity of their already existing brand.
