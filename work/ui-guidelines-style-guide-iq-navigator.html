IQ Navigator UI Style Guidelines

IQNavigator needed comprehensive UI Standards developed for their online presence, including (but not limited to) their online applications, current and future to create a consistent look and feel. These guidelines were developed based on their Brand Standards and recent website redesign.

While some of these applications were rebuild from scratch had much flexibility, others offered merely superficial style adjustment possibilities. By leaving out a lot of the website design elements and primarily focusing on the typography and the usage of colors for the elements we can still achieve the unified look desired.

Designed and developed in collaboration with Justin Stollsteimer under contract with Fusionbox, Inc.
