Data Visualization

With the launch of Intelivideo University the basic knowledge of running and growing an online video business is made readily available. The resulting, a big self-education resource chock-full of helpful articles to empower content owners to start selling successfully.

Break it down. And then some more.

In collaboration with the Marketing Strategist I reduced the amount of text in the provided copy to make it faster to read and also better suitable as infographics and eBook visuals. By pinpointing the core message of each article the main focus of the visual is established, everything else is deemed secondary.
